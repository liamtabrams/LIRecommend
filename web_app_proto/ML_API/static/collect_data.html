What are good values for accuracy and error?

There is inherent subjectivity in the application of the rating system and everyone will have their own unique corner cases which make modeling difficult. However, based on experiments of a user's drift over a 2-month period, the upper bound for accuracy is around 65% while the lower bounds for mean squared and absolute error are around .35. This means that a model achieving at least 60% accuracy and MAE and MSE's of less than .5 is doing quite well.
